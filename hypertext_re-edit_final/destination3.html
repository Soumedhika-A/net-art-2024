Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the state capital of Telangana, India, is a charming old city mixed with contemporary development. There was an explosion of colors, sounds, and smells in the vibrant bazaars, particularly around the famous Charminar. Irani tea and Hyderabad's well-known biryani have left a lasting cultural impression. My trip there was genuinely exceptional because of the city's fusion of Hindu and Muslim cultures, historical sites, and IT centers. Being a local, I took the opportunity to indulge in extensive traditional clothes shopping.
