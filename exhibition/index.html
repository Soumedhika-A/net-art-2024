Visual Tribute in K-pop

Exhibition Curated by Soumedhika Arkala

This exhibition, "Visual Tribute in K-pop," showcases the colorful combination of K-pop and digital fan art and the limitless imagination of fans all around the world. K-pop is not just a musical genre but a global phenomenon that creates unique digital deceit. The pieces of art selected for this exhibition show the thematic and aesthetic connections within K-pop culture, as well as the powerful emotional resonance and stunning visual impact of this musical phenomenon. By bringing together digital fan art from different artists, this exhibition aims to highlight the creative use of digital platforms for artistic expression, the collaborative attitude of the K-pop community, and how these works contribute to the ongoing dialogue between fans and the K-pop universe. Three art works showcase scrapbook, romantic, and fanfiction vibes.


Title: “PNG PACK NCT 2020 | RESONANCE PT2 - DEPARTURE”

Year: 2020

Artist: orangx

Description: A use of a playful, scrapbook-like aesthetic with elements such as cutouts, speech bubbles, and checkerboard patterns.


Title: “#301 PNG PACK [NCT Mark + Haechan]”

Year: 2020

Artist: ungodlybee

Description: a dreamlike, bubble-like frame, this artwork is a loving portrayal of NCT members Mark and Haechan. It features a gentle color palette that enhances their affectionate contact.


Title: “Yangyang ( NCT )”

Year: 2022

Artist: loriana98

Description: Using a dark color scheme and striking red accents, the artwork portrays NCT member Yangyang in a chic, melancholic profile that exudes an air of edgy sophistication.
